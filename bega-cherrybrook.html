Day 12: Bega -> Cherrybrook

January 3, 2014
Scroll Down

Decided not to go via Canberra – good choice.

Drove through Tilba Tilba and on to Central Tilba where we bought some cheese and prunes in port. Mum thought the prunes were a bit too potent so ended up putting them in a carrot cake.

Drove on to Narooma. Bought pies in Narooma and our special drinks. Lots of traffic going into Moruya. Drove on to Bateman’s Bay and then to Ulladulla for lunch. Had lunch on the breakwater rocks.

Went through Nowra and Kiama. Wrong petrol in Kiama so put in a little bit and got some more up the road, and ice blocks.

Decided not to take the M7.

Visited grandfather who was in the dining room waiting for dinner.

Got some groceries for tea after Dad parked as far away as possible.

No more earplugs, yippee.

THE END
