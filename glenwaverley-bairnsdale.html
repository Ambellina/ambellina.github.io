Day 10: Glen Waverley -> Bairnsdale

January 1, 2014
Scroll Down

Left the motel and drove to Bairnsdale.

Stopped in Rosedale for morning tea. Bought custard tarts and a banana and cream tart for morning tea.

Arrived at Bairnsdale a bit early to check in so we went to the park. Soon after we arrived every man and his dog turned up. Didn’t get to go on the zip line.

Checked in to the Bed and Breakfast and had a quick nap on the most comfortable beds on our trip. Beautiful old house restored (built in 1890 or earlier). Our room had a king and a queen bed. It was air conditioned but we simply opened the window to let in the breeze.

Mum thought that it was a bit spooky because of one of the pictures on the wall. She also thought that we might have been one of their first customers.

We were going to have a go of putt-putt but it had started to rain so we went to Paynesville for a drive. Saw the ferry that went across to the island. Saw a boat that looked like a guitar and a houseboat that had a lot of furniture in it but was open at the end. Stopped near the water and watched some paddle boarders and jet skiiers.

Dropped in at Eagle Point on the way back and Mum and Dad went for a mini-paddle.

Went to the Grand Terminus Hotel for dinner. I had chicken breast stuffed with feta and chorizo. Mum had cutlets and Dad had mussels and linguine. Mum played the poker machines while Dad and I watched the Greyhounds.

Back at the B&B I had a spa and it was awesome.
