Day 1: Cherrybrook -> Mollymook

December 23, 2013
Scroll Down

We left home at 8 o’clock.

Fantastic navigating from Dad saw us going quite out of our way to Macquarie Park then eventually through Bankstown and down to Wollongong.

We stopped in at Aunty Bev and Uncle Fred’s for a visit – we had a look around their place (very nice), had some banana cake and Christmas pudding. Invited to go on Fred’s boat sometime. Travacalm!

Sussex Inlet turn off to Berrara to see Uncle Gary’s place. Saw kangas like statues. Gary’s was nice, not yet to lock-up. Drove around to the caravan park to pick a spot for lunch at the inlet. Nice sandwiches home-made. Ant attack. Toilet big enough for a party. Dad and I went for a quick walk.

Got on our way to Mollymook. Checked in, nice big rooms, pool, hammock. Then went for drive to Lake Conjola and saw Big 4, water park and golf course, more kangas. Then to Milton to check out the shops. Couldn’t get a nice heart dress in my size :( Took a photo of my face in a wall.

Fish restaurant closed at 3pm, weird.

Target for undies, on sale!1

Fish and chips for tea, fish was really nice. Had a game of cards.

Earplugs worked well but made my ears a bit sore.

1 - This was significant, as I had forgotten to pack any undies!
