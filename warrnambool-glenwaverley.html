Day 8: Warrnambool -> Glen Waverley

December 30, 2013
Scroll Down

Couldn’t find the keys but the motel lady found them for us in the Bathroom haha

Drove down to the beach and I had a quick paddle. Tried to wash my feet but it didn’t work because the tap was for filling up water bottles. Drove past the lake park where I went on a couple of slippery dips and me and Dad walked across the rickety bridge.

More sightseeing and stopped at the suspension bridge.

Drove past the Botanical Gardens. Saw the Warrnambool race track. Visited the boat house and Granny’s grave – the first white woman to be buried in Warrnambool. Saw a ginger rabbit which smiled while I took its photo.

Got on the road to Glen Waverley. Had lunch at the volcanic lake (Bullen Merri lake) with all the flies. Drove through Camperdown which was nice.

We thought that the whole trip was on the highway but the first part was shit house – the second bit wasn’t much better. Did get to drive past some nice lakes, one of which looked salty.

By-passed Geelong and drove on to Melbourne. Luckily the bridge wasn’t still closed.

Arrived at GW by about 2.30. Had a bit of a rest before heading to Frankston.

Wanted to go for a swim but by the time we arrived it was too cold. Went to see the sand sculptures including Angry Birds, Harry Potter, the Hungry Caterpillar, Mr Men but the best was probably Where the Wild Things Are.

Decided not to have dinner at F and drove back to GW and got Chinese.
