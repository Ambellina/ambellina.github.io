Day 4: Traralgon -> Melbourne

December 26, 2013
Scroll Down

We got up. Had brekkie and got dressed. Left about 8:15 after Dad had a great chat with some old bloke who wanted to know all about the Jag’s fuel efficiency.

Stopped at Moe for the first wee. Had a quick look around town, saw the racecourse.

Stopped at Nar Nar Goon for second wee. I found 2 four-leaf clovers but Dad couldn’t find any.

Nice 5-lane highways, and no-one seems to be speeding or changing lanes too much.

Expertly directed when we got to Melbourne. Nowhere to stop out front in Collins St so we drove around the corner to Elizabeth St and parked in 30-min stop, put a little money into the meter so had to Amazing-Race style to get everything done in time. Dad and I took some bags - I knocked his wallet out and it splayed his money all over the street haha walked to the hotel and went up to level 8 to reception. Luckily the room was ready so we went to check it out on level 4. It was the furthest away from the lift, typical. My bed wasn’t there, so Dad went to ask the man about the bed and if we could get a closer room while I went to check on Mum to see if she was OK. I put more money in the meter then got more luggage and put it in the room - the bed would arrive later in the arvo, but no luck on switching rooms.

Went for a drive to St Kilda. Had a look at the marina, boat hotel, drove up along the main road but couldn’t see much of the beach, and it started to rain. Drove out to Albert Park and saw the different sport fields and Black Swans. Downpour as Mum went to use the toilet. Drove back to St Kilda and parked (best park ever).1

Saw a spot for lunch that had Christmas meals. Went to buy some cakes (apple pie slice, coconut slice, yellow meringue). Back to get Mum and we went to Barzar Lounge Restaurant (Carlisle Street) for lunch - the Christmas menu we’d seen on the window wasn’t there anymore but I asked the man and he said it was OK. We got the 2-course with wine special for $39.90 each. There is the table we sat at by the pillar.

Mum and Dad had Chorizo and Prawn kebabs for entrée with salad. I had the zucchini and eggplant parmigiana, it was amazing. Quite a big size, it was filling. Everyone liked their entrée. For main, Mum and I had the maple glazed ham with garlic mashed potato, broccoli and other veggies. Dad had the Barramundi with prawns, calamari and salad. We all had a glass of Moscato.

Drove back to Melbourne and I miscalculated the best street to drop Mum off and she had to walk an extra block :( I ran after her but she was too quick and halfway to the bottom of the street before I got there. Walked with her to the hotel and let her up the lift, then went back to meet Dad and find the carpark. Took a while, couple of times around the block until we worked it out. Took ages turning the corner because people kept walking out in front of cars trying to turn then they’d miss the lights.

Parked and walked back to the hotel. Had a quick nap then went shopping. Bought a $10 red cardi-top from some random shop, waterproof jacket and bottles from Kathmandu, and a dress from Myer. Dad bought nothing. Worst shopper ever. I was frustrated with all the people and the lines – I don’t really like shopping at the best of times but it was worse today. Lots of bargains though!!

Myer window was nice. Lots of street performers.

Got a frozen coke and walked back along the street opposite Federation Square. The weather had changed and it was getting quite chilly and windy.

Saw lots of people coming from the cricket. Stopped at Subway to get a tuna sub for dinner for Mum and I – Dad wasn’t hungry. Think we saw the Barmy Army – definitely saw lots of hooligans being stupid crossing the road and nearly getting run over. Stopped at Coles for a few supplies, then Liquorland for a bottle of Moscato.

Back at the apartment caught up with Mum and had a shower then dinner. Had a game of cards then our cakes.

The apartment is quite big and has everything we need except a tea towel haha. We’ll need to buy some washing powder so we can do the washing tomorrow.

I forgot to eat my pudding again!!

1 - Was I being sarcastic? Looks a bit far from the gutter!
