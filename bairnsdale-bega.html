Day 11: Bairnsdale -> Bega

January 2, 2014
Scroll Down

Breakfast was good but they forgot Mum’s crumpets. Plenty of cereal and juice. Went to the park and had our go on the zip line.

Had morning tea in Orbost after we got petrol. Had to wait for ages partly because of a motor bike rider who pushed in and was slow as an old mole at a christening. Had a vanilla slice, banana bread and a pasty in the park. Dad knows an old woman who once went for a tramp in the park.

Drove on to Cann River and swapped the driving.

Drove across the border to Eden. Nice beach and lookout. Read about the killer whales that used to herd the other whales into Two Fold Bay for the settlers to harpoon. The settlers would share the lips and tongues with the killer whales.

Had lunch at the park and then I drove us to Bega while Dad expertly navigated. Me and Mum stupidly had a nap and we missed going to the Bega cheese factory. Dove around Bega which was boring. Drove to Tathra where me and Dad had our first swim while Mum waited in the car. The water was lovely (says Dad) and a bit cold (says me).

Drove back to Bega hoping to get fish and chips for dinner but couldn’t find any so me and Mum resorted to KFC. Dad chose noodles. Dad bought some Coke for tea and some juice for my breakfast.

We played cards and Mum became president after a superbly played game.
